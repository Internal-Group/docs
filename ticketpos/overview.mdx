## What TicketPOS does

TicketPOS powers ticketing flows for events and venues inside your Roblox experience.

You use it to:

- **Sell tickets** – one-time, recurring, or time-limited access.
- **Validate entry** – control who can enter gates, doors, or regions.
- **Manage entitlements** – grant or revoke access levels such as VIP or staff.

TicketPOS runs fully inside your Roblox game via the `AG | TicketPOS` folder.  
You do **not** need to manage HTTP requests or API keys to use TicketPOS itself.

## Core concepts

- **Event** – a concert, session, or experience where tickets are required.
- **Ticket type** – combinations of access and pricing (for example, General, VIP, Staff).
- **Entitlement** – the actual access granted to a player for a specific event.
- **Gate** – a check-in point such as a door, portal, or zone.

## High-level architecture

At a high level:

1. You install the `AG | TicketPOS` content into your Roblox place.  
2. UI or physical gates call the `API` object inside `AG | TicketPOS`.  
3. The TicketPOS scripts manage ticket records and entitlements.  
4. Your game listens for events fired by the `API` to allow or deny access.

If you later introduce Atlas for licenses, TicketPOS entitlements can complement those licenses, but TicketPOS core flows remain event-driven inside Roblox.
