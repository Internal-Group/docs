## What is Aware?

Aware is a suite of tools for running experiences and communities safely and reliably:

- **RestaurantPOS** – point-of-sale for in-experience restaurants and concessions.
- **TicketPOS** – ticketing, check-in, and entitlements for events and venues.
- **EasyAPI** – a Roblox Lua helper that lets you call Aware APIs from your scripts.
- **Dynaban** – risk scoring and automated moderation (kick and ban).
- **Atlas** *(coming soon)* – license and entitlement portal for managing player access.

Use this documentation to go from zero to production for each product and to integrate the underlying APIs.
