## What RestaurantPOS does

RestaurantPOS powers ordering and payment flows for restaurants and concessions inside your Roblox experience.

You use it to:

- **Define locations** – restaurants, bars, kiosks, and other points of sale.
- **Manage menus** – items, categories, modifiers, and pricing.
- **Operate registers** – in-world terminals or UI that staff or players use.
- **Track orders** – from placement through preparation to completion.

RestaurantPOS runs fully inside your Roblox game via the `AG | RestaurantPOS` folder.  
You do **not** need to manage HTTP requests or API keys to use RestaurantPOS itself.

## Core concepts

- **Project** – your Aware project that groups all RestaurantPOS and TicketPOS data.
- **Location** – an individual restaurant or stand in your experience.
- **Register** – a specific checkout surface (for example, “Front counter #1”).
- **Menu item** – a purchasable item (for example, “Burger”, “Soda”).
- **Order** – a single transaction created when a player checks out.

## Typical architecture

At a high level:

1. You install the `AG | RestaurantPOS` content into your Roblox place.  
2. UI in Roblox collects order details and calls the `API` object inside `AG | RestaurantPOS`.  
3. The RestaurantPOS scripts handle order state and persistence.  
4. Your game grants items or experiences based on events fired by the `API`.

If you also use Dynaban or Atlas, those use Aware HTTP APIs and EasyAPI, but RestaurantPOS itself is event-driven inside Roblox.
